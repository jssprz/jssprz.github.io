Ph.D. candidate Jesus Perez-Martin

jeperez@dcc.uchile.cl

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Department of Computer Science, University of Chile and Research Assistant at Millennium Institute for Foundational Research on Data (IMFD). My current research interests are Computer Vision, Multimedia Information Retrieval, Computational Linguistics, Vision and Language, and Machine Learning. I am working on applications like video captioning and video generation from texts. Other of my research interests are the object, face, and action recognition.
