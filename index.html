Ph.D. Student Jesús Pérez-Martín

jeperez@dcc.uchile.cl

I am a Ph.D. researcher at the Computer Science Department, Faculty of Physical Sciences and Mathematics, University of Chile. My current research interests are Computer Vision, Multimedia Information Retrieval, Computational Linguistics, Vision and Language, and Machine Learning. I am working on applications like video captioning and video generation from texts. Other of my research interests are the object, face, and action recognition.
